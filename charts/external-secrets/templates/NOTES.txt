External Secrets Operator installed.

CRDs are managed by the upstream chart when `external-secrets.installCRDs=true`.

Optional bootstrap resources in this wrapper:
- `vaultStore.*` -> creates `ClusterSecretStore` or `SecretStore`
- `externalSecret.*` -> creates `ExternalSecret` that syncs into target Kubernetes `Secret`

For first install, keep `vaultStore.enabled=false` and `externalSecret.enabled=false` until Vault details are ready.
